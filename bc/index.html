Blueprint for Disruption in Mexico

Assumptions

To begin to conceptualize what a disruptive business model would look like for the Mexican market, it is important to clearly specify the following assumptions that will be made throughout this paper.

  • The value of data depends on the way you use it: Data has intrinsic value to businesses only to the extent to which it is used. Two companies can have the same amount of data related to sales, but, ceteris paribus, the company that obtains more actionable insights from that data will be better tasked to produce cheaper or better. Throughout this paper I will refer to these techniques, even if it is a hand-made table, as analytics. Thus, data without analytics is worthless.
